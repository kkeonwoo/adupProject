Chapter 1.
Responsibilities and Obligations to Customers

Our customers are the foundation of our business, and their views and opinions will always be highly regarded and respected. We strive to earn solid trust by continuously creating new value for them. 1. Respect Customers
LG Code of Ethics
We value the opinions of our customers and consider their needs our obligation. They will always have a place in the center of our decision-making process and business strategies.
 2.Create Value
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We work hard to create value for our customers. Through customer satisfaction, we can build the foundation of our sustainable prosperity.
(2) We continuously create value that benefits and improves our customers’ quality of life.
3.Deliver Value
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We are always true to our customers and will continue to be so.
(2) We offer excellent quality products and services at reasonable prices and respond promptly to our customers’ requests.

Chapter 2.
Fair Competition

In all our businesses worldwide, we gain competitive edge through fair competition and respect for local laws and regulations. 1. Pursue Free Competition
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We uphold the principles of the free market economic system and pursue open competition around the world. We earn our customers’ trust through top-quality products and services.
(2) We compete fairly and do not sabotage our competitors’ interests, nor exploit their weaknesses.
2.Comply with Laws and Regulations
LG Code of Ethics
We conduct our domestic and overseas businesses in strict accordance with local laws and regulations as respect for local business customs.

Chapter 3.
Fair Transactions

All business transactions abide by the principles of fair competition with equal opportunity for all entities. We build trust and cooperative relationships through fair and transparent transaction for long-term mutual growth. 1.Equal Opportunity
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We offer equal opportunities to all qualified companies seeking partnership with us.
(2) We register and select all applications in a systematic procedure, undergoing a fair and objective evaluation process.
2.Fair Transaction Process
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We conduct all business transactions as fairly. The terms and conditions of the transaction, as well as procedures, are thoroughly communicated between the parties involved.
(2) We do not engage in any form of unfair transaction using status or power.
(3) We exchange transaction information through appropriate procedure in a timely manner. Transaction standards are evaluated on a regular basis and improvements are made under mutual agreement.
3.Pursue Mutual Growth
LG Code of Ethics
(1) We support long-term growth with our dealers and vendors by enhancing their competitiveness through technological and managerial support. We share the profits derived from business innovations.
(2) We collaborate with business partners in promoting constructive business environment and fair business transaction.

Chapter 4.
Basic Ethics of Employees

LG employees must first set righteous values based on honesty and fairness, then accomplish their mission through continuous self-development and fair performance. 1.Basic Ethics
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG employees take pride in the company and maintain honesty and fairness at all time.
(2) LG employees, with high standards for morality, continuously strive to maintain their dignity and the company’s honor.
2.Accomplish Mission
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG employees accomplish their duties in accordance with the visions and policies of the company.
(2) LG employees carry out their assigned duties in the fairest manner possible while observing applicable laws and regulations.
(3) LG employees protect and preserve company property and maintain confidentiality on information gained during employment.
(4) LG employees openly communicate and cooperate with colleagues and relevant departments to increase work efficiency.
3.Self-development
LG Code of Ethics
LG employees establish standards for a role model employee and proactively engage in self-development to meet those standards.
 4.Fair duty execution
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG employees carry out duties with honesty and fairness, while seeking for opportunities to foster a respectful business culture.
(2) LG employees do not accept any form of financial compensation from stakeholders, that may negatively influence fair judgment.
(3) LG employees do not engage in immoral or unethical behavior both inside and outside of work.
5.Avoide Conflict of Interest with the company
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG employees avoid personal behavior or relationships that may cause conflict to the interest of the company.
(2) LG employees do not use company property to pursue their personal interest.

Chapter 5.
Corporate Responsibilities for Employees

All employees are respected as dignified individuals. LG encourages all individuals to freely express their creativity and fairly compensates high performance and achievements. 1.Respect Humanity
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG treats each employee with trust and values every individual’s dignity.
(2) LG gives employees a sense of ownership through work to increase self-fulfillment and satisfaction.
(3) LG continuously develops regulations and training guidelines to support employees perform their duties in a fair manner.
2.Fair Treatment
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG offers equal opportunity to all its employees based on their abilities and talents.
(2) LG evaluates the abilities and performance of its employees fairly and rewards them accordingly.
3.Promote Creativity
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG strives to create a working environment conducive to creative thinking and autonomous behavior.
(2) LG actively supports employees’ self-growth and adopts a long-term plan for human resources development.
(3) LG respects individual’s privacy and, through mutual trust, creates a more mature corporate culture.

Chapter 6.
Responsibilities to the Nation and Society

To protect the interest of shareholders, enhance the quality of life for citizens, and contribute to the growth of society, LG works towards growing into an influential company through reasonable business practices. 1.Rational Business Operation
LG Code of Ethics
(1) LG conducts its business respecting local and international values.
(2) LG pursues its business expansion based on stable corporate growth.
2.Protect Shareholder Returns
LG Code of Ethics
LG protects its shareholders’ interests by earning healthy profits through effective management.
 3. Contribute to Society
LG Code of Ethics
LG contributes to the nation and society through job creation, tax contributions, and cultural/ welfare programs.
 4. Conservation of the Environment
LG Code of Ethics
LG makes great effort in preventing environmental pollution and employs various means necessary to preserve the natural environment.

LG AI Ethics Principles

Going beyond technology, we strive to create a better life for all.
We observe the AI Ethics Principles to ensure a healthier and more sustainable society

  • Humanity

    인간존중
    • LG AI creates value for
      humanity and society
    • LG AI does not violate human rights
    LG prioritizes customers and operates under its people-oriented management principle. in every process of developing and utilizing AI, people come first. LG ensures that its AI will only be utilized to create positive value for the society and no human rights will be compromised.

  • Fairness

    공정성
    • LG AI treats all people fairly while respecting diversity
    • LG AI avoids unfair discrimination against individual characteristics
    LG upholds Jeong-Do Management, and respects all characteristics and diversity of individuals, equal opportunities, and fair treatment. LG sets AI standards that represent fairness and strives to prevent unfair discrimination against individual characteristics such as gender, age, and disability.

  • Safety

    안전성
    • LG AI operates in
      a safe and sturdy manner
    • LG AI predicts and responds to potential risks
    LG delivers excellent quality products and services to stay trustworthy. No exception to AI ​​system, high level of safety and security measures are in place. For unexpected emergencies, LG commits to regular monitoring and management of potential risks.

  • Accountability

    책임성
    • Clear definition of role and responsibility for those involved in LG AI development and use.
    • Ensuring that LG AI performs as planned and designed.
    LG fulfills all responsibilities to the community and customers with
    a sense of ownership. Throughout the entire AI development and utilization process, all involved parties will strictly abide by their respective roles and responsibilities. LG adapts verification procedures to ensure that LG AI performs as intended.

  • Transparency

    투명성
    • Open communication for customers to understand information and data processed by LG AI
    • Algorithm and data are managed with transparency and processed under strict rules and standards.
    LG upholds Jeong-Do Management through strict work ethics and honest disclosure. Providing open communication for customers to better understand and trust AI is top priority. LG manages the AI system with transparency so that customers can trust the integrity of the algorithm and data.
